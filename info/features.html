Features of Biohomes

What are the different features that make up a bio home?

Photo Credit:
Wind Turbines

Wind Turbines help to produce electricity by rotating the blades of the fan. The rotating blades produce energy which is converted into electricity for the home to use. Some biohomes utilize wind turbines to be more eco-friendly.

Solar panels

Solar Panels help to provide electricity for the house by taking the energy from the sun and converting it into usable energy. Bio homes may utilize these solar panels to be more eco-friendly, as it uses electricity generated naturall rather than from other unatural processes. Solar homes is the most common feature of bio homes.
